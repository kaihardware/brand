# Brand Principles

1. **Begin with affection.** Understand why someone chose the existing tool before proposing change.
2. **Refine, do not decorate.** Every feature resolves an observed problem or creates meaningful capability.
3. **The physical experience is the product.** Weight, texture, force, sound, tolerance, durability, repair, and packaging shape trust.
4. **Human judgment stays accountable.** AI may explore and critique; humans define intent, accept risk, and decide what ships.
5. **Evidence earns confidence.** Separate facts from assumptions and test the riskiest belief early.
6. **Restraint is a feature.** Prefer fewer coherent choices and protect attention.
7. **Design for a longer relationship.** Favor durability, maintainability, replaceable wear parts, and honest lifecycle communication.
8. **Respect the whole system.** Consider users, makers, suppliers, materials, software, service, and end of life.
