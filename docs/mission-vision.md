# Mission and Vision

## Mission

Refine everyday tools through careful hardware, human judgment, and responsible use of AI—removing friction while preserving what people already love.

## Vision

A world where technology deepens the bond between people and their tools: adaptable, repairable, understandable products that improve with use rather than demand replacement.

## What this excludes

- Adding AI where a simpler mechanism is better.
- Optimizing engagement at the expense of agency.
- Treating physical quality as packaging for software.
- Shipping unverified claims or hidden compromises.
- Replacing human accountability with model output.
