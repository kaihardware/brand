# Brand Manifesto

## Refining the tools you love

The best tools earn a place in our lives. Their shape becomes familiar. Their weight becomes expected. They disappear into the work.

But familiar does not mean finished.

KAI begins with respect for what already works. We observe where attention is lost, where movement is wasted, where a small irritation repeats a hundred times. Then we refine the experience without erasing what made the tool worth loving.

We build hardware because the physical world matters. Materials, tolerances, sound, repair, and the feeling under a fingertip are not implementation details; they are the product.

We use AI because it can widen our search, accelerate learning, and expose possibilities. It does not choose what people should value. People frame the problem, judge tradeoffs, and remain accountable.

We favor evidence over novelty, restraint over feature count, and enduring usefulness over launch-day spectacle. Our work succeeds when technology recedes and the tool feels more yours.

**好きな道具を、もっと好きにする。**
