# Brand Repository Structure

`docs/` contains approved brand meaning and policy. `assets/` contains approved masters/exports with metadata. `.github/` contains brand-specific review workflow. Future `examples/` may show correct applications; future `legal/` may hold approved trademark guidance, but only after counsel review.

Do not organize by campaign or individual. Organize by durable artifact type, give every asset an owner and version, and archive superseded exports without deleting their history.
